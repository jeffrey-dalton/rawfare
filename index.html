About Jill

Hi there Raw Foodie Folks!
Over the years I have learned to "cook" an array of raw food goodness. After many years of using my family and friends as test subjects it became clear the easiest and most enjoyable path into a raw food lifestyle is through desserts. This has lead me to create RawFare and offer to the beautiful city of Tauranga the wonderful and highly enjoyable benefits of raw food. Truly a way to enjoy the sweet indulgence without all the guilt!
